This curry is called Kadai Paneer because this dish is authentically prepared in a ‘Kadai’ (kadhai), an Indian wok.
This Indian cookware ‘Kadai’ has steeper sides and thick bottom.
However, you don’t necessarily need a Kadai for making Kadai Paneer recipe, you can simply use any other wok or a regular cooking pan the way I cooked it.
